How to Play

Controls

  • Left / Right: Steer the Fox
  • Up / Down: Accelerate and Brake
  • Tap Spacebar: Short Jump
  • Hold Spacebar (Falling): Deploy Energy Wings to Glide!
  • P or ESC: Pause the game

Mechanics

  • The faster you go, the higher your Score Multiplier!
  • Warp Gates: In Endless mode, survive 500 blocks to enter the warp gate, earn a Star and continue your run in a new world! Becomes increasingly difficult.

Tiles & Obstacles

  • 🟦 Blue buttons: Springs - bounce high!
  • ⏫ Glowing arrows: Boost Pads - break the speed limit!
  • 🟥 Red: Lava - instant Death!
  • 🟨 Dusty: Slows you down drastically.
  • 🚪 Tunnels: Steer safely through them. Jumping inside will bonk your head. Jumping on top lands you on deadly spikes!
  • 🟪 Gems: Collect for Combo Points.
  • Hint: the level editor has some additional tiles you can explore!
